From North of Egypt

Find Route 145.  Route 145 crosses the Lehigh River at Treichlers.  Continue South on Route 145 from Treichlers Bridge for 3.5 - 4 miles until it intersects with Route 329.  Turn right onto Route 329 and continue for only about 500 feet and then bear right onto Spruce Street.  Continue on Spruce Street for approximately 2 blocks and then turn left onto Wilson Street.  The Church is the second property on the right with street parking.

From East of Egypt

Find Route 329 and continue West on 329 to the intersection with Route 145.  Cross over Route 145 and continue for only about 500 feet and then bear right onto Spruce Street.  Continue on Spruce Street for approximately 2 blocks and then turn left onto Wilson Street.  The Church is the second property on the right with street parking.

From South of Egypt

Find the intersection of Route 22 and 145.  Route 145 is also known as MacArthur Road.  Follow Route 145 North for 4 - 4.5 miles until it intersects with Route 329.  Turn Left onto Route 329 and continue for only about 500 feet and then bear right onto Spruce Street.  Continue on Spruce Street for approximately 2 blocks and then turn left onto Wilson Street.  The Church is the second property on the right with street parking.

From West of Egypt

Find Route 329 and continue East on 329 into Egypt.  At the traffic light bear left and continues on 329.  Continue for about 1000 feet until you see the Lutheran Church located on the left.  Prepare to turn left onto Wilson just past the church and immediately before ascending the steeper hill.  Continue for about another 1000 feet to the Whitehall Mennonite Church located on the left.
