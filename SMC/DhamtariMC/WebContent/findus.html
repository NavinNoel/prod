Directions

Sunderganj Mennonite Church is located on NH-43 in Dhamtari city and is just 1.6 km from Dhamtari Railway Station. To reach Sundarganj Mennonite Church from Dhamtari Railway Station head south-west on NH-43 towards Sihawa Chowk, from Sihawa Chowk take a slight left turn and get on to NH-43. Take a right turn from Makai Chowk and reach Ratnabandha Chowk. After crossing Ratnabandha Chowk take the second left lane to reach the church in Sunderganj.
