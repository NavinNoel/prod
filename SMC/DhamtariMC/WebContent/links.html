The Mennonite Church in India is one of four Anabaptist groups in India related to Mennonite World Conference. The others are the Mennonite Brethren Church in India, the Bharatiya General Conference Mennonite Kalisiya, and the Brethren in Christ. Please use the links below if you like to find out more.

Mennonite English Senior Secondary School, Dhamtari

This school is established and administered by the autonomous body of the Christian Minority Community and run by Mennonite Board of Christian Education, Dhamtari (CG). Situated at the heart of the city the school provides the best opportunity and environment to its students for studies and other co-curricular activities.

 Mennonite Central Committee

MCC seeks to demonstrate God's love by working among people suffering from poverty, conflict, oppression and natural disaster. MCC sends people, food and material goods to communities recovering from war and natural disasters.
Mennonite World Conference

Here you will find a window on churches and people from the global community of faith in the Anabaptist tradition. You will also find information about Mennonite World Conference through the work of its commissions and networks, and through its meetings and resources.

 Mennonite Missions Network

Mennonite Mission Network exists to lead, mobilize and equip the church to participate in holistic witness to Jesus Christ in a broken world.
